Initial Observations

* Larger homes generally sell for higher prices.
* Houses with higher overall quality tend to have significantly higher sale prices.
* Newer homes generally have higher sale prices than older homes.
* Garage area shows a positive relationship with sale price.
* Several columns contain many missing values that will need to be handled during data cleaning.
